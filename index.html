About Me

I am a self-taught professional Unity developer with over ten years of Unity experience, five of which was spent working with an award-winning advertising agency making cutting-edge applications and experiences for high-profile clients such as Royal Caribbean, Lysol and Invesco QQQ.

I am a jack-of-all trades with a wide array of hobbies and interests including sailing, filmmaking and carpentry — skills I love to apply in my professional work. I take pride in my modular style of code architecture, writing clean and extendable code designed to outlast the projects they’re made for and be repurposed for future endeavors.
